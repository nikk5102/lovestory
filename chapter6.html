When The Story Really Began

It had been one whole year since college ended.

No contact. No conversations. Nothing.

Just memories.

And then suddenly… you added me on Snapchat.

Tell me, how small is this world?

Out of all the people, after all that time, you?

And we started talking.

At first it was normal. Casual. Friendly.

I liked talking to you. Of course I did.

You were my crush. Somewhere inside me, that curiosity was still alive.

But I wasn’t thinking about boyfriend. I wasn’t thinking about anything serious.

I was running away from feelings. From attachments. From risks.

I just wanted to talk. To know you. That’s it.

But day by day…

We didn’t just talk. We started knowing each other.

Morning to night. Night to morning.

Whole day. Whole conversations. No breaks.

And then… that day came.

You remember, right?

Of course you do.

(I’m smiling while writing this.)

The day you asked me to be your wife.

Not girlfriend.

Wife.

Do you even realise what you did to my mind that night?

I didn’t sleep. Not till 7 in the morning.

I kept thinking — “Is he joking?” “Is he teasing?” “Is this serious?”

Because you love playing with my mind. Teasing me. Confusing me.

And I was stuck between my heart and my thoughts.

For the first time in my life…

I didn’t reject someone who had a crush on me.

And I had a crush on him too.

I just said, “I need time.”

Time to process. Time to understand.

But day by day, you became a habit.

You became my calm.

That one place where I knew — there is someone.

My heart was sure.

“Yes. He is the one.”

But my mind? Still confused.

And then… on propose day, you proposed again.

And this time… I said yes.

We didn’t even know how perfectly that timing aligned.

Coincidence? Or something bigger?

And then our first call.

Do you remember?

You were out with your friends. You couldn’t even tell them that you have someone now.

You went inside the car just to charge your phone and call me.

Eleven missed calls.

I was angry. But the moment I saw that…

My anger melted.

Because for the first time, I felt — this isn’t one sided.

And the way you tried to calm me, the way you didn’t give up…

It mattered.

The next day — 12-2-26.

Our first video call.

I was so shy before picking it.

But once the call started… time stopped.

1:40. And suddenly it was 7:15.

We forgot the world. We forgot time. We forgot everything.

Day by day, our conversations became deeper.

Our feelings became deeper.

We understood each other. We saw each other. Not just the surface — but the real parts.

I don’t know what love is supposed to feel like.

But I know this — I am very happy with you.

And I wouldn’t change a single thing about you.

Do you know how many times I watched your Valentine reaction?

Too many.

You don’t even know how madly I’ve fallen for you.

I blush randomly. I imagine us in little scenes. Movie moments. Cute things.

I see couples in series. I see small romantic gestures. I see my parents’ little things.

And I imagine — us.

I want to recreate all of it with you.

I want a home with you.

Maybe small. But ours.

Where every corner has our memories. Where happiness is simple. Where it’s just us and our love.

I want to see the world with you. Every joy. Every struggle. Every phase.

I don’t know what the future holds.

But I know this — in every moment, I want you.

You made me realise I deserve to be loved. To be someone’s priority.

With you, I fell in love with myself again.

And I don’t know how to measure how much I love you.

It’s not just “I love you.”

It’s — I choose you. I see you. I want you in my every tomorrow.

Maybe I’ve gone a little mad in your love.

But if this is madness…

I never want to be normal again.
